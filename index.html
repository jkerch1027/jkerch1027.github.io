About

Hi! I am currently a student working at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign with experience in both fusion plasma physics and condensed matter experimental work. Previous projects include data analysis for lithium evaporations into hydrogen plasma, experimental investigation of field-induced transitions in relaxor ferroelectric PMN-PT at different concentrations, and laboratory automation software (VEE Pro) for polarized light microscopy and in-situ vacuum measurements. Currently, I am very interested in electron transport properties and disordered systems.
